Em 2006, Marky Ramone gravou um CD ao vivo com a banda Tequila Baby, em que eles tocam alguns hits dos Ramones, entre eles, Rockaway Beach, Teenage Lobotomy, I Don't Care, Sheena Is a Punk Rocker, Pet Sematary, The KKK Took My Baby Away, I Don't Want You, Beat on The Brat, Poison Heart, I Believe in Miracles e Blitzkrieg Bop.
